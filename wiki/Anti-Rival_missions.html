Anti-rival missions

Anti-rival missions[1] are missions against the rivals of a country.

The Rival of our Rival
 此信息可能已落后版本，最后更新于1.23
----

The rival of our rival is $COUNTRY$, so they should be our friend.

 
类型： Rivals of rivals
触发
触发任务条件

The target country:

  • is not a rival of this country.
  • has an opinion of at least 0 towards this country.
  • has an opinion of less than 50 towards this country.
  • is not at war with this country.

This country:

  • 是独立国家或朝贡国
  • has knowledge of the target country.
  • is not a rival of the target country.
  • has not completed successfully this mission during the last 10 years.
 触发任务机会

Base weight: 1000

Modifier:
  • This country has a ruler with a diplomatic skill of at least 6: ×2
  • The target country has a ruler with a diplomatic skill less than 3: ×0.5
成功条件

The target country has an opinion of at least 100 towards this country.

失败条件

This country 是除朝贡国以外的附属国
At least one of the following is true for the target country:

  • is at war with this country.
  • is a rival of this country.
  • has declared this country to rival.
  • does not exist.
效果
奖励

This country gains

  • 5 prestige.
  • 25 diplomatic power.
 临时效果

无

Trade missions

 此内容可能已落后版本，最后更新于1.23

Trade missions[3] are missions to strengthen the trade of a country.

Gain Trade post in China
 此信息可能已落后版本，最后更新于1.22
----

Our merchants have a need for control of the Chinaware trade. We need to make sure we control at least one province in Asia producing Chinaware.

 
触发
触发任务条件

This country:

  • is not at war.
  • has its capital in Europe.
  • has at least 6 home ports.
  • does not have provinces producing chinaware.
  • has discovered a coastal province within its colonial range in South China.
  • does not have the modifier “Chinaware Trade”.
  • does not have completed successfully this mission in the last 40 years.
 触发任务机会

Base weight: 1000

Modifier:

This country:

  • has completed the naval idea group: ×1.15
  • has completed the expansion idea group: ×1.10
  • has completed the exploration idea group: ×1.05
  • owns at least 1 province in Asia: ×1.12
成功条件

This country owns at least 1 province producing chinaware.

失败条件

This country has no home ports.

效果
奖励

This country gets the modifier “Chinaware Trade” for 10 years with the following effect:

  • +5 tax income.
 临时效果

无
