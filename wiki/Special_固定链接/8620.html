"Expanded Terra Firma" missions

Conquer Friuli

 此信息可能已落后版本，最后更新于1.21
----

The city of Venice by virtue of its location is fairly safe from attack. However to avoid being at the mercy of the shifting Italian political landscape we must expand our land holding.

 
触发
触发任务条件

The country:

  • is  威尼斯.
  • 是独立国家或朝贡国
  • does not own Friuli (111).
  • does not have the modifier “Expanded Terra Firma”.

The owner of Friuli (111):

  • owns at least 3 provinces if Friuli (111) is a capital.
  • is not an ally of Venice.

This mission can be completed successfully only once.

触发任务机会

Base weight: 3000

Modifiers:

Venice has a ruler with a military skill of at least 4: ×2

成功条件

 威尼斯 owns Friuli (111).

失败条件

 威尼斯 是除朝贡国以外的附属国

效果
奖励

 威尼斯 gets the modifier “Expanded Terra Firma” for 10 years with the following effect:

  • +10% national tax modifier.
 临时效果

 威尼斯 gets a claim on Friuli (111).

Other missions

Subjugate Aquileia

 此信息可能已落后版本，最后更新于1.21
----

The Patriarch of Aquileia was once one of the most powerful states in the Italian peninsula. Now its days of glory are over and Venice should supplant it.

 
触发
触发任务条件

The country:

  • is  威尼斯.
  • is not a subject nation.
  • does not have the modifier “Military Expansionism”.

 阿奎莱亚:

  • is not a subject nation.
  • is a neighbor of Venice.
  • has less cities than Venice.
  • has a total development of less than 50.
  • is not a free city.
 触发任务机会

Base weight: 3000

Modifier:
  • Venice has an opinion of less than 0 towards Aquileia: ×2
成功条件

 阿奎莱亚 is a vassal of  威尼斯.

失败条件

Any of the following is true:

  • Venice 是除朝贡国以外的附属国
  • Aquileia does not exist.
  • Aquileia 是除朝贡国以外的附属国
  • Venice does not have the Subjugate casus belli on Aquileia.
效果
奖励

 威尼斯 gets the modifier “Military Expansionism” for 10 years with the following effects:

  • +1 yearly prestige,
  • +10% manpower recovery speed.
 临时效果

 威尼斯 gains the Subjugation casus belli on  阿奎莱亚, valid for 25 years.

Disrupt Genoese Trade

 此信息可能已落后版本，最后更新于1.21
----

Blast! Genoa has managed to secure free trading rights through the Bosphorus. We are losing markets fast. Either we monopolize trade in Liguria, or we knock out their Black Sea Colonies before they know what hit them.

 
目标省份

All provinces in Crimea (area) which are owned by Genoa.

触发
触发任务条件

The country:

  • is  威尼斯.
  • 是独立国家或朝贡国

 热那亚:

  • is not a subject nation.
  • is not allied with  威尼斯.
  • has done the mission ‘End the Sound Toll’.
  • owns Azov (286), Caffa (285) and Liguria (101).
 触发任务机会

Base weight: 3000

Modifier:

Venice has the idea ‘National Trade Policy’: ×2

成功条件

Venice owns all target provinces.

失败条件

Venice 是除朝贡国以外的附属国

效果
奖励

 威尼斯 gains “Black Sea Trade Concession” in the Constantinople trade node for 10 years worth +5 trade node power.

临时效果
  •  威尼斯 gains a claim on every target province.
